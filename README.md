# BOW_The_Aftermath 
Short for Brink of War: The Aftermath. A mod I made for HOI4 simulating what happens if Italy never joined the Second World War.
<br><br>
Current mod version: v0.1.1
<br>
Codename: Mare Nostrum

## Description
It has been 2 years since the fall of Fascist Germany, but Europe never stays dormant. The USA has introduced interventionist policies after their new president was sworn in. The Italians and their allies have been waiting for this moment to show that Europe still has a fighting chance and that their time of mourning won't be used as a chance for outsiders to gain influence in Europe. The Soviet Union decides its time to spread communism and does that with the eastern european states as well as Communist China, which is fighting a civil war. There are three major powers, who will you pick?
